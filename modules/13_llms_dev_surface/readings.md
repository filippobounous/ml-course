# Readings — Week 13

## Primary

- **Anthropic** — *Claude Code documentation* (CLI reference, settings, hooks, MCP, skills).
- **Model Context Protocol spec** — `modelcontextprotocol.io` — architecture, tools, resources, sampling, transport.
- **Zheng et al. (2024)** — *Judging LLM-as-a-Judge with MT-Bench and Chatbot Arena*. Quantifies judge variance, position bias, length bias. Essential for honest evals.
- **Dubois et al. (2024)** — *AlpacaEval 2.0: A Simple Way to Debias Automatic Evaluators*. Practical bias-correction for LLM judges.

## Supplementary

- Yao et al. (2022) — *ReAct*. (Re-read, this time with an LLM-backed policy rather than the Week-11 hand-written one.)
- Reynolds & McDonell (2021), *Prompt Programming for Large Language Models*.
- Chase & contributors — *LangChain / LangGraph* source. Read the tool-calling primitives, not the tutorials.
- Anthropic (2024) — *Building effective agents* (blog post) — good taxonomy of orchestrator / parallel / router / evaluator patterns.

## Stretch

- Perez et al. (2022), *Red Teaming Language Models with Language Models*.
- Huang et al. (2024), *A Survey of Autonomous LLM Agents*.
